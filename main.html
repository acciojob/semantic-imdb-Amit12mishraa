Summary

Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly convicted of murder, is sent to Shawshank State Penitentiary. Over many years he forms a friendship with fellow inmate Red and quietly plans his escape, using patience, intelligence, and small acts of hope to survive and ultimately win his freedom.
